Hi! I'm Emma Coton, an aspiring business owner who likes to dabble in a few other things along the way. I'm 19 years old, though soon to be 20, and I am currently living in Lehi, Utah where I am attending BYU Provo. I live at home with my parents and my three sisters. Sometimes it's a hectic life in my household, but I wouldn't trade it for anything else in the world. Family is one of the key things in my life, so I would never wish putting something else in it's place.

Since we're here, let's go into a little more detail about me. Beyond being someone who aspires to own a business one day, I also take high interest in drawing, writing, baking, outdoor activities like camping, hiking, archery and roller-blading. I love going on trips and spending time with my family even if we don't go out all that much vacation wise. Though I'd love to go to the beach, I am pretty okay staying home and doing my own thing.



It's the small things in life that keep me going
